Our Approach to New Product Development

From Ingredients to Finished Products

  • 1.

    Crafting the Journey

    We transform raw ingredients into finished chocolate products through precise handling and controlled processes such as tempering, refining, and moulding. Each product is engineered to deliver consistent performance across batches.

  • 2.

    Sourcing Ingredients

    Ingredient quality plays a critical role in final product performance. We source reliable cocoa derivatives and functional ingredients that support flavour clarity, stability, and regulatory compliance.

  • 3.

    Product Development & Recipe Formulation

    Every formulation is developed with a clear understanding of manufacturing behaviour, shelf stability, and cost efficiency. Recipes are tested, refined, and validated before commercial production.

  • 4.

    Collaborative Development

    We work closely with your team to align technical decisions with brand intent. From texture refinement to flavour balance, collaboration ensures the final product reflects your brand promise.

  • 5.

    Flexibility & Speed

    Our manufacturing systems are designed to adapt quickly — whether it is scaling volumes, adjusting formulations, or accommodating packaging changes — without compromising product quality.

  • 6.

    Beyond Launch

    Our involvement continues even after commercial launch. We support ongoing optimisation, repeatability, and future product extensions to help brands grow sustainably.

Co-Creation & OEM Partnership

Co-creation is central to how we work. Each project is treated as a unique collaboration, balancing technical feasibility with brand differentiation. By combining structured processes with creative flexibility, we enable brands to bring distinctive chocolate products to market efficiently.
