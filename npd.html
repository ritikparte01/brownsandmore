Specialised Chocolate NPD Capabilities

Advanced development for specialized chocolate products

Sugar-Free Chocolate Development

Developing sugar-free chocolate requires precise formulation control. We work with polyols, fibres, alternative sweeteners, and blends to achieve:

  • Balanced sweetness curves
  • Proper viscosity and flow
  • Clean taste profiles without aftertaste

Dairy-Free / Plant-Based Chocolate

We create dairy-free chocolate that delivers creaminess and indulgence without milk ingredients.

Through careful flavour and rheology engineering, we ensure smooth mouthfeel and familiar taste using plant-based systems.
